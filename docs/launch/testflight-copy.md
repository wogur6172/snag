# Snag TestFlight Copy

Copy-ready text for the first iOS beta.

## Beta Name

Snag

## Short Invite Line

Turn real life into collectible stickers.

## Beta App Description

Snag turns things around you into collectible cutout stickers.

Capture a pet, object, meal, sign, desk item, or friend moment, clean it up, and place it into your collection. You can also create shared boards where friends drop little real-life moments together.

This beta is focused on the core loop: capture, cut out, collect, and share a board.

## What To Test

Please try:

- Making your first Snag from the camera.
- Making a Snag from your photo library.
- Cleaning up a cutout with manual erase.
- Moving, resizing, and rotating Snags in a category.
- Creating a new category.
- Creating a shared board.
- Joining a shared board with an invite code.
- Adding Snags, drawings, or text to a board.
- Inviting one friend to test the board with you.

## Known Beta Limits

- Cutouts may miss edges, hair, transparent objects, or low-contrast backgrounds.
- Shared boards may not feel fully real-time yet; some changes may appear after a short delay.
- Snag is iOS-first for this beta.
- Free beta limits are intentionally small: 2 created boards, 3 joined boards total, 8 members per board, and 60 Snags per board.

## Feedback Prompts

Use these questions when asking testers for feedback:

- Did you understand what to do in the first minute?
- What did you try to Snag first?
- Did the cutout/refine step feel fun or annoying?
- Did the collection feel worth coming back to?
- Was inviting a friend clear?
- What felt slow, confusing, or awkward?
- What would make you send this to a friend?

## TestFlight Review Notes For Apple

Snag is a creative collection app. Users can capture or select an image, cut out the subject, and place it into a personal collection. Users can also create invite-code-based shared boards with friends.

The app does not require a traditional email/password login for the beta. If App Review needs to test a shared board, create a board from the Social tab or use an invite code provided in the review notes.

Core review path:

1. Open Snag.
2. Tap the camera button.
3. Allow camera permission.
4. Capture an object or choose an image from the library.
5. Confirm the cutout.
6. Place it into the collection.
7. Open Social.
8. Create a board or join with an invite code.
9. Add a Snag, drawing, or text to the board.

Social safety features:

- Board owners can remove members.
- Board ownership can transfer when needed.
- Users can leave boards.
- Board content can be deleted by the user in the app.
- A support contact will be provided in the App Store metadata and privacy policy.
