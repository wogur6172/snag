# Snag US Positioning

## Core Position

Snag is a playful collection app that turns real life into collectible stickers.

It is not just a background remover. It is not a productivity organizer. It is a light social collecting toy for things people notice, love, and want to keep.

## One-Line Options

- Turn real life into collectible stickers.
- Collect the little things around you.
- Snap it. Cut it out. Keep it.
- A camera roll for things you actually want to play with.
- Make a shared board out of real-life moments.

Recommendation for beta:

```text
Turn real life into collectible stickers.
```

## Target Wedges

### Pet People

They already take too many photos of cats and dogs. Snag gives those photos a playful second life as stickers in a collection or shared board.

Example message:

```text
I started collecting my cat like stickers.
```

### Desk And Comfort Object Collectors

People keep little objects, charms, cups, books, bags, plushies, keyboards, and daily carry items because they feel personal. Snag makes that collection visual and playful.

Example message:

```text
Everything on my desk became collectible.
```

### Friend-Group Inside Jokes

Friends collect expressions, food, random signs, small moments, and jokes. A shared board lets them drop those moments into one playful space.

Example message:

```text
We made a board of dumb little moments.
```

## What To Avoid

- Do not lead with "AI background remover."
- Do not sound like a serious photo editing app.
- Do not over-explain the technology.
- Do not make the brand feel too childish.
- Do not promise perfect cutouts.

## Brand Voice

Casual, short, visual, and a little mischievous.

Good:

- "snag this"
- "drop something here"
- "collect your world"
- "tiny things count"
- "make a board together"

Avoid:

- "optimize your memories"
- "organize your visual assets"
- "AI-powered workflow"
- "seamless productivity"

## App Store Subtitle Ideas

- Collect real-life stickers
- Capture, cut out, collect
- Make shared sticker boards
- Turn moments into stickers

Recommendation:

```text
Collect real-life stickers
```

## App Store Description Shape

Start with the feeling before the feature:

```text
Snag turns the things around you into collectible stickers.

Take a picture of a pet, object, meal, sign, or friend moment. Snag cuts it out, lets you clean it up, and drops it into your collection. Create categories for your own little world, or make a shared board where friends can add moments together.
```

Then list features:

- Capture or choose photos.
- Cut out the subject.
- Manually refine edges.
- Place, resize, and rotate Snags.
- Organize categories.
- Create shared boards with invite codes.
- Add drawings and text.

## Organic Marketing Strategy

Start organic before paid ads. The product needs proof that people understand the first minute and want to invite friends.

First channels:

- TikTok
- Instagram Reels
- YouTube Shorts

Why:

- The product is visual.
- The before-after moment is easy to show.
- Friend-board videos can become the invite loop.

Do not start paid ads until:

- TestFlight public link is ready.
- First-install flow is stable.
- At least 10-20 real testers have tried it.
- We know which message gets people to make their first Snag.

## First Metrics To Watch

- Install to first Snag.
- First Snag to saved collection.
- First session board creation.
- Board creation to invite shared.
- Invite code join success.
- Returning next day.
- Qualitative feedback: "I know what I want to collect."
