# Snag Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Snag is an iPhone app that lets you capture or choose photos, cut out subjects, organize them into collections, and place them on shared boards with friends.

## Information Snag Handles

Snag may process photos or images that you capture or select in the app.

Personal collections are intended to stay on your device unless you choose to save, share, or add content to a shared board.

When you use shared boards, Snag may store:

- Cutout images added to shared boards
- Text and drawings added to shared boards
- Board names and invite codes
- Board membership and owner/member roles
- Board content placement, size, rotation, and display settings
- A nickname you choose for shared board display

Snag may ask for access to Camera and Photos so you can capture, choose, and save images.

## How Information Is Used

Snag uses this information to:

- Provide capture, cutout, collection, and shared board features
- Sync shared boards between invited members
- Show nicknames and member lists in shared boards
- Improve reliability and respond to support or deletion requests

Snag does not currently use third-party advertising tracking.

## Shared Boards

Personal collections are private to your device unless you use a sharing feature.

Shared board content is visible to people who have joined that board. Only add photos, drawings, or text that you are comfortable sharing with other board members.

## Service Providers

Snag may use:

- Supabase, for shared board database, storage, and anonymous app identity
- Apple TestFlight and App Store services, for beta distribution, crash reports, and tester feedback
- Expo Application Services, for building and submitting app binaries

These providers process information according to their own policies and Snag's configuration.

## Data Retention And Deletion

Local collection data stays on your device until you delete it in the app or delete the app.

Shared board data may remain stored while the board exists or while needed for app functionality, safety, abuse prevention, or support.

You can delete Snags, leave boards, delete boards when allowed, change your nickname, and revoke Camera or Photos permissions in iOS Settings.

## Children

Snag is not intended for children under 13.
