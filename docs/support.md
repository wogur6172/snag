## Common Questions

### Why Does Snag Ask For Camera Access?

Snag needs Camera access so you can capture something and turn it into a cutout sticker.

### Why Does Snag Ask For Photos Access?

Snag needs Photos access when you choose an image from your library or save a cutout to Photos.

### Are Personal Collections Uploaded?

Personal collections are intended to stay on your device unless you choose to use a sharing feature.

### What Are Shared Boards?

Shared boards let invited people place Snags, drawings, and text together on one board.
